Sara Scarella graduated as professional dancer from M.A.S academy of Milano, Italy. Continued the studies in Budapest with the Budapest Dance theater company. Winner of a scholarship for deepening even more the formation in New york city. In Israel she attended Vertigo dance company international program and Batsheva outstanding dancers program. Later joined Kolben dance company of Jerusalem.

Now freelancer in Berlin. Dance teacher of ballet and contemporary dance. Yoga teacher, certified as: Personal Yoga trainer, Vinyasa yoga and Yin yoga by Yoga alliance international.
